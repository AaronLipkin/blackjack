Blackjack

Rules

Primary Goals

We all know BlackJack. It is a game between the dealer and player to get closest to 21 without going over. Click on the chips to set your bet.

Other Rules

Dealer Logic

The dealer's play is automatic. The dealer will always hit on anything 16 or below and stand on anything 17 or higher.

Double Down

If your starting hand equals 9, 10, 11 you can double your bet. You then get one and only one more card.

Naturals

If your starting had equals 21, this is called a BlackJack. If you have this, you immediately recieve three times your inital bet and the round is over. If the dealer has this you immediately forfeit your bet. If you both have it the game is a wash.
