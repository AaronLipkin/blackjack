Rules

Primary Goals

We all know BlackJack. It is a game between the dealer and player to get closest to 21 without going over.

Other Rules

Double Down

If your starting hand equals 9, 10, 11 you can double your bet. You then get one and only one more card.

Naturals

If your starting had equals 21, this is called a BlackJack. If you have this, you immediately recieve three times your inital bet and the round is over.
